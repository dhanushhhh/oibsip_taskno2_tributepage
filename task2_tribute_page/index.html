Mahendra Singh Dhoni

One of the greatest cricket captains of all time, also known as Captain Cool and widely regarded as India's #1 captain.

His Achievements

He led India to victory in the ICC T20 Cricket World Cup in 2007.

Received the Padma Shri, one of India's highest civilian awards, in 2009.

Under his captaincy, India won the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011.

Dhoni captained India to win the ICC Champions Trophy in 2013.

Honored with the Padma Bhushan, another prestigious civilian award, in 2018.

As captain of Chennai Super Kings, Dhoni secured 5 IPL titles.
